Baked Beet and Potato Chips

Jan 30, 2015 • Shanna Patel

I’m an unapologetic grazer. Waiting 5 hours between meals ain’t for me. As a result, I try to make sure that what I’m snacking on is wholesome, unprocessed and ideally homemade. In all honesty, once I tried baking my own vegetable crisps/chips, store bought versions paled in comparison. These crisps/chips are super easy to make. Keeping it simple with just a pinch of salt and freshly ground black pepper means you can really taste the vegetables.

You will need a mandolin for this recipe.

Ingredients

2 potatoes 2 beetroots Olive Oil Sea Salt

Method

  1. Preheat your oven to 400F
  2. Wash and peel your vegetables
  3. Using the thin slicing setting on your mandolin, slice your vegetables into discs.
  4. Place the slices in a bowl and dress the vegetables with a glug of olive oil until they are very lightly coated.
  5. Evenly season the vegetables with the salt and black pepper.
  6. Place the vegetables on a non stick baking tray or a tray lined with baking parchment and lay them all out. They can be close to one another.
  7. Depending on their size they'll cook within 20-30 minutes. Keep an eye on them. Beets take longer than potatoes so after 20 minutes, rotate the tray and let them bake for 10-15 mins more.
  8. Once out of the oven, leave them on the tray to cool and crisp up.
