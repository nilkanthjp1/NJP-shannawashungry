Vegan Crêpes

Feb 17, 2015 • Shanna Patel

Crêpes are super versatile. With a basic sweet crêpe recipe, you can be as imaginative and adventurous as you like with toppings and fillings. Some ideas:

  1. Nutella
  2. Roasted banana and Nutella
  3. Warm fruit compote
  4. Just a little lemon juice and powdered sugar.
  5. Fresh strawberries and flaked almonds
  6. Stewed apple with cinnamon

I didn’t think crêpes would be easy to replicate without egg, but these ticked all the boxes. They’re delicately thin and therefore lighter than having a stack of pancakes. They’re slightly sweet, soft in the middle and crisper at the edges. Give them a go!

Ingredients

Yield: makes 6 crêpes 3/4 cup all purpose flour 2 tsp brown sugar 1/2 tsp baking powder 1 tsp neutral oil, such as sunflower 1 pinch of salt 1 cup almond milk Oil for cooking (optional - if you have a good non stick pan you may not need any)

Method

  1. Combine all your ingredients together in a bowl. Whisk until smooth.
  2. Heat an oiled non stick frying pan over a medium heat.
  3. Pour one small ladle of batter into the centre of the pan and gently twirl the pan to spread the batter around the pan to make a larger circle.
  4. Cook for 2 minutes, until the top side of thecrêpe is totally dry.
  5. Flip the crêpe and cook for 2 more minutes until slightly golden.
  6. Serve warm with choice of filling.
